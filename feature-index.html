Kouign Amann
Pastry of the Month, Feb 26, 2018

Say hello to the Kouign Amann. Queen of my heart and the stuff your dreams will be made of after you taste a good one. Pronounced queen-ya-mahn, this little baby hails from Brittany, France’s northwestern most region. It comes in all sorts of shapes and sizes and isn't always much of a looker but this one is all about the feel.

General Porpoise
Bakery of the Month, Feb 25, 2018

General Porpoise - a unique stop for your next donut fix! This Capitol Hill shop offers delicious Malasada-style donuts with artisan fillings. You’ll find flavors like chocolate marshmallow, vanilla custard, and lemon curd in addition to two more rotating flavors that are sure to keep you coming back from more. General Porpoise also offers a selection of fine quality coffees and teas, sourced locally.
